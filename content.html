Grow With Us

At Blue Bottle, we never stop learning. Here are just a few of the educational opportunities we offer.

 

Barista Training

Our multi-day barista training curriculum is the best in coffee. Combining brew theory with hands-on practice, it equips everyone from novices to experts with everything they need to know to brew and serve specialty coffee.

Origin Trip

Every year we award an elite group of Blue Bottlers a free trip to visit one of our coffee-growing regions, such as Guatemala or Colombia. The members of the group are all nominated by their peers, in testament to their work.

Career Development

We offer impactful development opportunities and career paths for anyone who is excited to grow as a leader in their cafe and at Blue Bottle more broadly by learning and developing in coffee, food, cafe operations, and people development and management.

Cultivate

For all our staff and markets, we offer monthly free seminars and talks featuring in-house coffee experts as well as partners in specialty coffee, such as importers and coffee farmers.

Our Guiding Principles

Whether we’re making a pour over or designing a new digital experience, our actions are inspired by a set of five principles that guide us in our mission to connect the world to delicious coffee.

 
 
Guiding Principles
Welcome Everyone

People come first. Without hospitality, we cannot exist. We create spaces where people belong. Everyone has a place at the table: Guests, team members, and suppliers.
Raise the Bar

From seed to cup, we set the standard in everything we do—and drive constantly to do it better. We are self-starters who think creatively to solve complex problems, recognize our mistakes, and learn from failures.
Build for Tomorrow

We think long-term. We’re savvy with our resources, saving where we can to invest in what matters: A sustainable future for our people, the company, and our planet.
Simplify

Just as our cafes eliminate barriers between guests and delicious coffee, we remove obstacles in our work. We stay nimble, communicate clearly, and show a bias towards action.
Grow Together

Like coffee needs water, we need one another to bloom. Collaboration takes work, so we practice it like a craft. We listen carefully, share feedback openly and honestly, remain transparent, and inspire each other with kindness and humor.
