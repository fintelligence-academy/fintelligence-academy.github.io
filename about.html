Our Story

Fintelligence & The Fintelligent Young Scholars

The adoption of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence continues to progress from a niche activity to mainstream applications at an ever-accelerating pace in this Big Data era. Here at Fintelligence, we are proactively embracing the ML in finance.

We are a group of young scholars in finance and computer science community across the globe. We gather around to talk about the future of financial markets and quantitative investment, and we want to share the exciting academic achievements with everyone. There comes Fintelligence academy, an open-source community to promote data science in finance.

Principles of Fintellgience

It is all about idea inspiration and knowledge sharing of data-centric finance. We aim to promote the emerging state-of-the-art academic findings in finance and machine learning community - the two languages we both specialize in.

Open-source is our spirit. Not a fun of the dark secrecy surrounding the investment industry, we look forward to sharing and wholeheartedly open discussion of the exciting breakthroughs in quantitative finance.

Actionable - indifferent to publication, we aim to only share the research that is trustworthy and actually works in financial market. With a tactful industry practitioner taste, we sift the quality research that is impactful and truly value-added to the real finance world. To be sure, we impartially discard all overfitting and "good results bias".

Theory - classical finance ideas never fade but evolve. Having been rigorously trained in economics and finance theory, we embrace you with the reliable data-era quantitative research that also has well-grounded economic intuition.

We hope you will enjoy our research.

Connect with US from Anywhere across The World

We are eager to hear about others' thoughts. Please do not hesitate to reach us via email: fintelligence.group@gmail.com or WeChat group message. We also have a WeChat discussion group.

Our people are the kindest, most hardworking and talented from a diverse quantitative background. We’re an impossibly eclectic group of prestigious university PhD candidates, top investment bank, asset managers, and hedge fund quantitative researchers, and all-around dreamers. Join us if you are also enthusiastic about machine learning and quantitative finance.
